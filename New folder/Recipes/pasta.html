Burrito Recipe

Make a Burrito In Five Minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 1/2 cup canned refried beans
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream

Steps

  1. Warm the tortilla. Place the tortilla on a microwave-safe plate and heat it in the microwave for about 10 seconds or until it becomes soft and pliable.
  2. Spread the refried beans. Take the warm tortilla and spread the refried beans evenly across the center.
  3. Add the cheese. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese on top of the refried beans.
  4. Add salsa and sour cream. Spoon the salsa and sour cream over the cheese layer. You can adjust the amount according to your taste preferences.
  5. Roll the burrito. Fold the sides of the tortilla inward, then roll it tightly from the bottom to form a burrito shape.
